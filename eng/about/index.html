Experience our vision



Life's Operating System™

Asurion is the leading consumer electronics protection, and support service provider globally; partnering with the world's largest carriers and retailers. With offices in North America, the United Kingdom and Asia Pacific, we serve over 150 million subscribers globally with 50 million of those in Asia. For the past 20 years, we've helped people simplify their digital lives. We restore. We solve. We keep them connected.

For the past 20 years, Asurion has helped people across the globe balance the interdependency between life and technology.

Today, as the industry-defining leader of technology solutions, we ensure 300 million consumers' devices and appliances stay online and on the job in this fast-moving, tech-driven world.

We help you retrieve the seventeenth (and perfect) picture of your baby in that sweater, and make sure the undeliverable email is delivered. We replace your phone, and make your TV shake hands with your device. We're the silent partner in your video editing career, and the reason you become more productive with your technology.

We're the people who know what that one button does. And sometimes what it does not do.

We have always worked as though life depends on us. And, increasingly, it does. Technology is virtually everywhere. It drives performance at work, powers a growing number of tasks at home, and guides us as we move between the two.

At Asurion, we not only understand technology, we appreciate its value. Life without our laptops, devices, and apps we have can become very challenging, very quickly. And as your home's devices become interconnected, the stakes become even higher.

We're a technology company. But we're also an amazing group of people. When you call us, you'll talk to people with the patience and experience to genuinely help. We understand what it's like to lose contact with your friends, family and business partners and we understand the value of the content on your device. We replace. We restore. We solve. We keep you connected.

Asurion ensures you are in-sync with your technology. And your life is in balance. We do so globally, by speaking many languages, and by working across any device, platform or provider.

There's only one system like ours on earth, and it took two decades of innovating, and empathizing, to build.

Asurion. Life’s Operating System™.
